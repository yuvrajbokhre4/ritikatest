Integra Magna

Integra Magna is a strategic design agency that creates the digital product and brand experiences that elevate companies to their next exciting chapter.

Project Overview:

As part of my internship at Integra Magna, I was tasked with redesigning the company's website. Previously known as IM Designs, the agency's old website reflected its early days with a creative but cluttered aesthetic. With the rebranding to Integra Magna, the company sought a more professional, minimalistic, and clean design that aligned with their evolved brand identity and market positioning as a mature and sophisticated design agency.

Role: UX/UI Designer

Company: Integra Magna

Duration: 6 Months

Challenges:

The main challenges included:

Outdated Design: The old website was cluttered and did not reflect the current professionalism of the agency. Brand Inconsistency: The previous website did not align with the new branding and positioning of Integra Magna.

User Experience: The navigation was not intuitive, making it difficult for users to find information quickly.

My Approach:

Competitive Analysis:
To ensure the new website met industry standards and stood out among competitors, I conducted a thorough competitive analysis.

This involved:
Identifying key competitors and analyzing their websites for design trends, usability, and content strategy.
Noting the strengths and weaknesses of competitor sites to inform our design decisions.
Understanding the visual language and user experience expectations within the design industry.

User Needs:
Interviewing Co-founders within the company to gather insights on what they wanted the new website to convey.

Target Audience:
Identifying the primary audience for the website and understanding their needs and preferences.

Design Trends:
Keeping up-to-date with the latest web design trends and best practices for a clean, minimalistic approach.

Design Process:
Wireframing and Prototyping Based on the research and analysis, I created wireframes and prototypes:

Wireframes:
Focused on layout and structure to ensure content was organized logically and accessibly.

Prototypes:
High-fidelity prototypes to visualize the final design and gather feedback from stakeholders. Visual Design

Conclusion:

The new website for Integra Magna successfully achieved the following:

Improved User Experience: Enhanced navigation and usability, making it easier for visitors to find information.

Modern Aesthetic: A clean, minimalistic design that reflects the agency's professionalism and sophistication. Brand Alignment: A visual identity that aligns with the new brand direction and market positioning of Integra Magna.
